TRON HACKATRON | Season 7 | Entry by Techtek

The Memes on Sunpump are small pieces of art and a snapshot of our time and culture! With miniMEME, you can display them on a small inexpensive device to turn your cupboard into a mini meme art gallery, showcasing the latest and greatest Tron memes. It is a bit like a digital picture frame that we all know, but with a twist because it got cool Tron blockchain features! Get ready to experience some unexpected fun!

miniMEME is an open-source IoT project made by Techtek for the Tron Blockchain. It will work on ESP32-S3 chipsets and will work on inexpensive boards like the USD 15,- Lilygo S3 and other ESP32 (S3) boards.

Read more about miniMEME in the Devpost introduction and download the latest version on Github, here you can also find how to get started. Join the miniMEME community on Discord or Telegram.
